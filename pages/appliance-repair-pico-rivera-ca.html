About Pico Rivera

Pico Rivera sits in southeast Los Angeles County, formed where the historic communities of Pico and Rivera came together along the San Gabriel and Rio Hondo rivers. About 61,500 people live here, and the city keeps a strong community identity anchored by a handful of well-known local spots. The Pico Rivera Sports Arena, open since 1979, is the largest Mexican rodeo ring in the United States and draws crowds all year. Neighbors catch a show at the Pico Rivera Centre for the Arts, opened in 1989, and browse local history at the Pico Rivera Historical Museum, housed in a former train depot built in 1887. The distinctive circular Pico Rivera Library has served readers since 1961, and families spread out across the city's seven neighborhood parks and the Pico Rivera Municipal Golf Course. That everyday mix of established single-family homes and updated kitchens is exactly the range of Viking, Dacor, LG, and Whirlpool work we handle here.
