Star Burner Ignition, Inverter Boards, and Thermador's Failure Patterns

Thermador appliances are premium products with specific, well-documented failure modes across each product line. Here is what we diagnose most often in Orange County.

Star Burner Ignition Failure The 5-point Star Burner electrode wears or becomes fouled, causing one or more burners to fail to light. The distinctive star-tip design can accumulate grease in the crevices between points. We clean and test before recommending electrode replacement.
Hot Surface Light Stays On When the cooktop's residual heat indicator stays lit after full cooldown, the temperature sensor or relay has failed. This is a safety fault that prevents the indicator from clearing correctly. We diagnose the specific component before any replacement.
Oven Control Panel Failure The Masterpiece and Pro Harmony oven displays fail with age, presenting as a blank screen, unresponsive buttons, or incorrect temperature readouts. Control panel replacement is a half-day job that requires the correct panel assembly for the specific model year.
Sapphire Dishwasher Drain Pump Failure Standing water at the end of a cycle or a fault code indicating drain failure on Sapphire and Star-Sapphire dishwashers. The drain pump is the most common hardware failure on these models. We test the pump before recommending replacement.
Door Latch and Interlock (Dishwasher) The dishwasher door appears closed but the cycle does not start, or the door releases mid-cycle. The door latch interlock micro-switch fails after many open-close cycles. Replacement restores normal function in most cases same-day.
Freedom Column Inverter Board Failure The compressor inverter board on Freedom column units controls motor speed and is the most expensive common failure on these refrigerators. When it fails, the unit stops cooling. We confirm the board failure through diagnostics before recommending a replacement.
Convection Fan Motor Failure Masterpiece and Pro Grand ovens develop convection fan motor wear over time. The first sign is a grinding or rattling noise during bake cycles, followed by uneven heating as the fan slows. Fan motor replacement restores consistent oven performance.
Touch-Glass Induction Control Failure CIT and CET series induction cooktops develop unresponsive touch zones or complete panel failure. The touch-glass assembly integrates the display and control electronics. Replacement requires careful alignment to the cooktop frame.

Thermador's Star Burner and Freedom Column: Engineering That Shapes the Repair

The Thermador Star Burner's 5-point star geometry creates more surface area around the flame ring than a conventional round burner, which is why it heats more evenly, but the star-shaped grate also creates five tight crevices where grease collects. When a Star Burner fails to light, the first step is never a parts order: it's determining whether the tip is fouled or physically worn. We clean the electrode and test ignition on every Star Burner call before we recommend a replacement.

The Thermador Freedom Column refrigerators use an inverter-driven variable-speed compressor rather than the fixed-speed units found in most refrigerators. The inverter board adjusts compressor speed based on thermal demand, which is more energy-efficient, but it also introduces a failure mode that fixed-speed units don't have. When a Freedom Column stops cooling, the inverter board is often the cause. These boards are model-specific and expensive; we confirm the fault through diagnostics before recommending the replacement.

Thermador's Sapphire dishwasher uses condensation drying rather than a heating element; it relies on the stainless steel interior absorbing residual heat from the wash cycle. This is more energy-efficient but it means drying performance depends on the door sealing correctly at the end of the cycle. A worn door latch on a Sapphire doesn't just cause a no-start fault; it affects drying performance well before it presents as a full interlock failure.
