Common Appliance Problems We Fix

Whether it is a refrigerator that stopped cooling overnight or a washer that won't spin, we diagnose fast and fix it on the first visit.

  • Refrigerator not cooling or freezing unevenly
  • Washer not spinning, draining, or filling
  • Dryer not heating or taking two cycles to dry
  • Dishwasher not draining or leaving dishes dirty
  • Oven not reaching temperature or heating unevenly
  • Gas stove burner not igniting or staying lit
  • Freezer running too warm or building up ice
  • Microwave not heating or sparking inside
  • Garbage disposal humming but not spinning
  • Wine cooler not cooling or showing error codes
  • Ice maker not making ice or leaking
  • Washer making loud banging or grinding noises
