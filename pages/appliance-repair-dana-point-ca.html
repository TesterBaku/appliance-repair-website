About Dana Point

Dana Point sits on the south Orange County coast between Laguna Beach and San Clemente, with Laguna Niguel and Mission Viejo just inland. The city is defined by its harbor, where the Ocean Institute and the tall ship Pilgrim sit beside the marina, and by the bluff-top resorts at Salt Creek Beach, the Waldorf Astoria Monarch Beach Resort, and the Ritz-Carlton. Families spend weekends at Doheny State Beach, the revitalized Lantern District, and the bluff trails above Strand Beach. That blend of gated estates in Monarch Beach and Ritz Cove with harbor condos and older Capistrano Beach bungalows is exactly the range of Sub-Zero, Wolf, Thermador, and everyday Whirlpool and LG work we handle here week to week.
