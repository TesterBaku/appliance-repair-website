About Seal Beach

Seal Beach is a small, walkable coastal city in the northwest corner of Orange County, bordered by Huntington Beach, Westminster, and Garden Grove, with the Rossmoor community just to the north. It is anchored by the historic Seal Beach Pier, one of the longest wooden piers in California, the shops along Main Street, Eisenhower Park, the Seal Beach National Wildlife Refuge, and the Old Ranch Country Club, with Pacific Coast Highway running along the coast. Because the housing ranges from Old Town beach cottages to the large Leisure World retirement community, the appliances we repair here run the full spectrum: compact, apartment-sized units in Leisure World, salt-exposed washers and refrigerators near the water, and premium built-ins in the College Park and waterfront homes.
