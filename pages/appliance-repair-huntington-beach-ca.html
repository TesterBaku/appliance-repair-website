Living near the coast? Here's what salt air does to your appliances.

Huntington Beach's ocean air carries salt and moisture that corrodes metal components faster than in inland cities. Refrigerator condenser coils collect salt deposits that reduce cooling efficiency. Dryer heating elements and drum supports oxidize. Dishwasher spray arms and door hinges corrode. Stainless steel exteriors pit near the water.

The fix isn't complicated: regular condenser coil cleaning, checking door gaskets, and addressing small symptoms early. But when a repair is needed, knowing the coastal context helps diagnose the root cause. Our technicians work in HB regularly and look for salt-related damage as part of every inspection.
