About Cypress

Cypress is a quiet, family-oriented city in northwest Orange County, bordered by Buena Park, Garden Grove, and Anaheim. Incorporated in 1956, it grew up around Cypress College and the green spaces of Arnold/Cypress Park, Oak Knoll Park, and the historic Forest Lawn Cypress memorial grounds. Families gather at the Cypress Community Center and along the Lincoln Avenue corridor, with quick access to the SR-22 Garden Grove Freeway and the I-605. Because so much of the housing dates to the same two decades, the appliances age in waves: original built-ins and decades-old refrigerators, washers, and ranges are the bulk of what we repair here, alongside the high-efficiency units in remodeled kitchens.
