About La Habra

La Habra is an established, family-oriented city in the northeast corner of Orange County, bordered by Brea, Fullerton, and Buena Park, with Placentia and Yorba Linda a short drive east. It is anchored by the Children's Museum at La Habra in the historic Union Pacific depot, the La Habra Marketplace, Westridge Golf Club, and parks like Portola Park and La Bonita Park, with the Imperial Highway, Harbor Boulevard, and Whittier Boulevard corridors running through town. Because the housing spans several decades, the appliances age in waves: original built-ins and decades-old refrigerators, washers, and ranges make up most of what we repair here, alongside the premium built-ins in the newer Westridge and Country Hills kitchens.
