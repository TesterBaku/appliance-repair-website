About Los Alamitos

Los Alamitos sits at the northwest corner of Orange County, bordered by Cypress, Seal Beach, and Garden Grove, with Long Beach just across the county line. Incorporated in 1960, it is one of the county's smaller and older cities, built up around the Los Alamitos Race Course and the Joint Forces Training Base. Many homes here are mid-century ranch houses from the 1950s, 1960s, and 1970s, which means original and once-replaced appliances are now well past their prime. Residents know the Old Town Main Street District, Laurel Park, Little Cottonwood Park, the Los Alamitos Medical Center, and the adjacent Rossmoor community. That mix of established single-family homes, downtown apartments, and the larger kitchens of Rossmoor is the everyday range of refrigerator, washer, dryer, oven, and dishwasher work we handle across the city.
