Evaporator Fans, Carborundum Igniters, and Frigidaire's Known Weak Points

Frigidaire appliances fail in specific, recognizable ways, and because Frigidaire is Electrolux-built on its own platform, the parts and faults are different from Whirlpool-family machines. These are the repairs our technicians handle most often on Orange County homes.

Refrigerator Not Cooling A warm Frigidaire refrigerator usually points to a failed evaporator fan motor, a stuck air damper blocking cold air to the fresh-food section, or a defrost-system failure that lets the coils ice over. Gallery models with inverter compressors can also suffer electronic control-board faults. We test the fan, damper, defrost heater and thermostat, and control board to find the exact fault, and carry common evaporator fans and boards on the van.
Gas Range Oven Not Heating A Frigidaire gas oven that will not heat has a failed carborundum (ceramic glow-bar) igniter in almost every case. The igniter wears out over time and stops drawing enough current to open the gas valve. Sometimes the oven temperature sensor drifts out of calibration. We meter the igniter's current draw and the sensor resistance to confirm the fault before quoting.
Dishwasher Won't Drain Standing water at the bottom of a Frigidaire dishwasher usually traces to a clogged drain pump, a blocked wash/filter assembly, or a kinked drain hose. Frigidaire dishwashers do not have a food disposer, so food debris can accumulate in the sump and block the pump inlet. We clear the blockage and confirm the pump works before returning the unit to service.
Front-Load Washer Door Boot Leak A Frigidaire Affinity or Gallery front-loader that leaks from the front door almost always has a torn or worn door-boot seal. The rubber bellows develops small tears over time, especially on Affinity models. We replace the boot and clamp, confirm alignment, and test a full cycle to verify no leaks remain.
Dryer Not Heating A Frigidaire dryer that tumbles but blows cold air usually has a failed heating element (electric) or a weak igniter (gas). The thermal fuse may also have blown if the vent is blocked. A restricted vent is the hidden cause of most no-heat calls because it causes overheating that trips the safety fuse. We check the heating circuit, vent, and fuses before quoting parts.
Electric Range Burner Won't Heat A dead burner on a Frigidaire electric range is usually a failed surface element or a bad infinite switch (the control knob). If only one burner is out, it is almost always the element; if multiple burners fail or the heat cannot be adjusted, the switch is the culprit. We meter both to find the exact failed part.

Is Frigidaire the Same as Whirlpool?

No, Frigidaire is not owned by Whirlpool. Frigidaire is owned by the Electrolux Group, the Swedish appliance giant that is the world's second-largest appliance maker. Electrolux builds Frigidaire alongside its own Electrolux-brand appliances on shared platforms, so a Frigidaire refrigerator and an Electrolux refrigerator share much of their sealed system, evaporator fan, and control-board architecture. That is very different from KitchenAid, Maytag, or Amana, which are all Whirlpool-family brands that share parts with each other.

That distinction matters the moment something breaks. A technician who stocks parts for Whirlpool-family machines will not carry the right evaporator fan, carborundum igniter, dishwasher drain pump, washer door boot, or control board for a Frigidaire; Frigidaire uses Electrolux parts, not Whirlpool parts. We carry Frigidaire-specific parts on the van because Frigidaire is one of the most common brands in Orange County homes, and we know the Electrolux platform well. That usually means a faster, one-visit repair on your Frigidaire appliance.

The practical upshot is simple: Frigidaire parts are widely available and reasonably priced because Frigidaire is one of America's best-selling appliance brands. Most Frigidaire repairs are affordable and worth doing. If you are not sure which Frigidaire model you own, read us the model number off the door frame or the tag when you call, and we bring the right Electrolux-family parts the first time.
