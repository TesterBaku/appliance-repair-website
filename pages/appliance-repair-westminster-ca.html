About Westminster

Westminster sits in the heart of northern Orange County, bordered by Garden Grove, Huntington Beach, Fountain Valley, and Santa Ana. It is best known as the home of Little Saigon, the largest Vietnamese-American community in the United States, centered on the Asian Garden Mall (Phước Lộc Thọ) and the Bolsa Avenue corridor. Families shop at Westminster Mall, relax at Sigler Park, and gather at the Westminster Civic Center, while Westminster Memorial Park anchors the city's west side. Because so much of the housing dates to the 1950s and 1960s, the appliances age in waves: original built-ins and decades-old refrigerators, washers, and ranges are the bulk of what we repair here, alongside the high-efficiency units in remodeled kitchens.
