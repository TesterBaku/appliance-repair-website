About Yorba Linda

Yorba Linda is a city in northeast Orange County, best known as the birthplace of President Richard Nixon. The Richard Nixon Presidential Library and Museum sits in the western part of the city near the original Nixon family home. Recreation options include Yorba Regional Park, a large regional park along the Santa Ana River, and Black Gold Golf Club at Vista del Verde. The Yorba Linda Country Club serves the western residential community. Retail and dining are centered at Town Center at Yorba Linda near Yorba Linda Boulevard and Fairlynn Boulevard. The city is primarily residential and is consistently ranked among the wealthiest cities in California by median household income, which tracks with the higher prevalence of luxury kitchen appliances we see on service calls here.
