About Corona

Corona sits in western Riverside County, known for decades as the "Circle City" after the historic Grand Boulevard that loops around its original townsite. About 157,000 people live here, where the CA-91, the I-15, and the CA-71 converge at the mouth of the Santa Ana Canyon. Shoppers and diners head to Dos Lagos and The Crossings at Corona, while Glen Ivy Hot Springs in nearby Temescal Valley has drawn visitors to its natural mineral pools since 1860. Families spread out across Santana Regional Park and take in the hills along Skyline Drive at the edge of the Cleveland National Forest. The city is also home to the historic Fender guitar factory, Corona Regional Medical Center, and the small Corona Municipal Airport. That mix of established downtown homes, hillside master-planned communities in South Corona and Eagle Glen, and newer construction out toward Temescal Valley means our Riverside technician works across every kind of kitchen and laundry room, from everyday Whirlpool and LG units to built-in Sub-Zero, Wolf, and Viking suites.
