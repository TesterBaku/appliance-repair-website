Common Appliance Problems We Fix

Most repairs are completed in a single visit. Here are the symptoms we diagnose every day across Brea.

Refrigerator not cooling or freezing unevenly
Built-in or panel-ready fridge making loud noise

Washer not draining or leaving clothes wet

Oven not heating or range burner not lighting

Garbage disposal humming but not spinning

About Brea

Brea sits at the northern edge of Orange County, bordering La Habra, Fullerton, Yorba Linda, and Placentia. It grew up around oil in the early 1900s, a history preserved at the Olinda Oil Museum and Trail, and today it is best known for the Brea Mall and the open-air Birch Street Promenade downtown. Families head to Carbon Canyon Regional Park for its redwood grove, the Brea Sports Park for weekend games, and the Tracks at Brea, a rail-to-trail path that runs through the heart of the city. Brea's housing splits between mature tract neighborhoods with original appliances and newer communities like La Floresta full of built-in units, which is exactly the range of repairs we handle here week to week.
